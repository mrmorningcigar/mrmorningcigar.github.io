About Mr. Morning Cigar

This is a personal ritual project built around morning stillness—coffee, a cigar, and reflection. It’s not about speed, flexing, or noise. It’s about showing up for your day with calm focus.

The Ritual (simple)

Step outside. Notice the light. Sip something warm. Light slowly. Breathe. Let the mind unwind. Write one honest sentence. That’s it.
